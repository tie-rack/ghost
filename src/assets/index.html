Ghost

Ghost is a social network without the social part or the network part. Since there's no one else, you can have any name you want. Just log in.

Nothing you post goes out to the internet. Nothing gets written to a database. Nothing sticks around. Everything disappears when you log out or close the page.

Relax... You're on Ghost.
